Welcome to Odyssey – Easwari Engineering College’s Space Club!

Who we are

Odyssey is an interdisciplinary technical club focused on fostering innovation and curiosity in the fields of aerospace and astrophysics. Founded on August 14th, 2024, at Easwari Engineering College, our mission is to empower students from diverse backgrounds to explore the vast realms of space science and technology.

Our vision

We believe in reaching for tomorrow. By combining knowledge from multiple disciplines, we aim to inspire the next generation of engineers, scientists, and space enthusiasts. Whether you're passionate about building spacecraft, studying celestial bodies, or unlocking the mysteries of the universe, Odyssey is your launchpad.

What we do

  1. Workshops and Seminars:
    Learn from industry experts and academic leaders in aerospace and astrophysics.

  2. Hands-On Projects:
    Collaborate on innovative projects, from satellite design to space mission simulations.

  3. Space Talks:
    Engage in discussions on the latest advancements in space exploration.

  4. Competitions:
    Participate in national and international space challenges to test your skills.

Join the Journey

Odyssey is more than just a club—it’s a community. Whether you're a curious beginner or an experienced space enthusiast, we welcome you to be part of this journey into the cosmos.
